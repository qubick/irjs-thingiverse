Summary

Hello Makers :)

I've get a task from guys of Fillamentum to print something for their amazing Vertigo Starlight material. When I was sitting with my laptop on the balcony with no idea how to make design fitted for this amazing Vertigo one my wife gave me a blueberry smoothie. Sometimes there are things which bring you ideas from nothing :D I hope that this design fits perfectly to this crazy material, what do you think about that?

Here it is. My Blueberry bowl.

Design was pretty easy to make, because the Autodesk Meshmixer helped me a lot in this case.

Model is adjusted to print without supports - There are some overhangs, but they are from the bottom and they are not vissible after printing. If you are still afraid of them, the multimaterial with soluble material is recommended here.

Model has also edited the bottom which has 1 mm thickness.

So enjoy guys and if you like it, leave me your comment, like, collect or donate which will help me to grow :)

Thanks,
Antonín
